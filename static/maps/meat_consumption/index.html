Meat and fish consumption in India

What?

  • This is a visualization of the per-capita consumption of meat and fish per month in India.
  • States are marked from lighter to darker based on the amount of item consumed in kg per person per month.
  • Move your mouse over each state to see the quantity of the curent item consumed there.

Source

This data is obtained from the National Sample Survey Organization (NSSO)'s report on Household Consmption of Various Goods and Services in India, 2011-2012, published in June, 2014.
